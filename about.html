About Our Resort

Explore

Step into luxury and comfort in our mountain resort apartments. Each space is meticulously designed to blend rustic charm with modern elegance. Warm wooden accents and cozy furnishings invite you to unwind after a day of adventure. Floor-to-ceiling windows offer panoramic views of the majestic peaks, bringing the outdoors inside. Curl up by the fireplace with a cup of hot cocoa or gather around the spacious dining area for a family meal. Our mountain retreat apartments are your perfect sanctuary amidst nature's grandeur.

Restaurant

Enjoy!

Our restaurant offers a cozy yet elegant dining experience with stunning views of the surrounding peaks. Our menu showcases locally sourced ingredients and seasonal flavors, expertly crafted into dishes that celebrate the essence of the mountains. From hearty classics to creative culinary delights, each bite is a journey of taste and tradition. Join us for an unforgettable dining experience that perfectly complements your mountain retreat.

The Ski Area

Most fun part!

Explore our mountain resort's ski area, offering diverse terrain for all levels, from gentle slopes to challenging runs. Enjoy breathtaking views, well-groomed trails, and cozy lodges for refueling. Whether you seek thrills or tranquility, our ski area promises an unforgettable winter adventure.
